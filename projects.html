LabCongo Projects

Our projects are presented in three simple groups: upcoming work, ongoing activities, and completed initiatives that support reliable mining analysis and local technical capacity.

Education Project

Modern School Laboratories for Early Scientific Training

LabCongo is preparing a program to equip schools with modern, practical laboratories so students can discover science, observation, measurement, and experimentation from an early age.

The project aims to strengthen local scientific culture by giving schools access to safe equipment, guided laboratory activities, teacher support, and learning environments that connect classroom theory with real technical practice.

By investing in young learners, LabCongo wants to help build a future generation of technicians, engineers, researchers, and responsible professionals able to contribute to the DRC's scientific and industrial development.
