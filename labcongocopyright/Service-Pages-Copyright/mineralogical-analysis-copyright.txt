Mineralogical Analysis
Identification and characterization of minerals to support exploration, processing, and metallurgical planning.
Mineralogical analysis helps explain how valuable minerals are hosted, associated, and liberated in a sample. LabCongo uses this service to support ore characterization, beneficiation studies, and technical decisions across the mining value chain.
By combining careful sample preparation with mineral observation and analytical interpretation, we help clients understand the mineral composition that influences recovery, processing behavior, and product quality.
What This Service Covers
Identification of principal and accessory minerals in ore, rock, concentrate, and tailings samples.
Assessment of mineral associations, texture, alteration, and liberation indicators.
Support for geometallurgical studies and ore variability programs.
Comparison of mineral composition across zones, pits, campaigns, or process streams.
Reports and Deliverables
Mineralogical description and interpreted findings.
Summary of dominant mineral phases and relevant associations.
Recommendations for complementary geochemical or metallurgical testing.
Clear technical report for project and laboratory records.
