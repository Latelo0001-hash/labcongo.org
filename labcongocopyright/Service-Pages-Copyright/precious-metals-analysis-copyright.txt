Precious Metals Analysis
Assay services for gold, silver, and other valuable metals in ores, concentrates, and process materials.
Precious metals require careful handling, representative preparation, and analytical discipline. LabCongo supports clients who need dependable assay information for gold, silver, and other valuable metals in mining samples.
The service is suited to exploration, production monitoring, concentrate evaluation, and verification programs where accuracy, traceability, and clear reporting are essential.
What This Service Covers
Assay support for gold, silver, and selected valuable metals depending on sample type and grade range.
Testing of ores, concentrates, tailings, process streams, and selected exploration samples.
Controlled preparation to improve representativity for precious metal analysis.
Use of quality-control checks to support reliable grade reporting.
Reports and Deliverables
Precious metal assay results with sample identification and units.
Analytical report with method notes and QA/QC information.
Support for comparison between feed, concentrate, and residue streams.
Recommendations for confirmatory or repeat analysis where needed.
