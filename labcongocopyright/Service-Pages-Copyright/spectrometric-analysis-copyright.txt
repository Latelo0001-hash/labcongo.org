Spectrometric Analysis
Instrumental measurement of major and trace elements for reliable mining and geochemical data.
Spectrometric analysis supports fast and precise measurement of elemental composition in mining samples. LabCongo uses this service for ores, concentrates, soils, rocks, and process materials where reliable elemental data is essential.
The service is designed to provide consistent analytical data, with appropriate calibration, instrument control, and verification steps to reduce uncertainty and improve confidence in reported values.
What This Service Covers
Measurement of major, minor, and trace elements depending on the sample matrix and project objective.
Support for exploration screening, grade control, concentrate evaluation, and process monitoring.
Instrumental analysis following suitable preparation and calibration procedures.
Quality checks using standards, blanks, duplicates, or internal controls as required.
Reports and Deliverables
Elemental result tables with sample IDs and units.
Method and instrument information in the analytical report.
Quality-control notes for validation and traceability.
Data files suitable for technical review and project databases.
