Quality Control (QA/QC)
Quality-control programs that improve confidence, traceability, and reliability of laboratory results.
QA/QC is essential for credible mining data. LabCongo helps clients design, apply, and review quality-control practices that support reliable analytical results and transparent decision-making.
This service can be integrated into analytical programs or delivered as a focused review of sample batches, controls, duplicate performance, contamination indicators, and data consistency.
What This Service Covers
Review of standards, blanks, duplicates, repeats, and internal control performance.
Support for exploration, grade control, metallurgical, and environmental datasets.
Identification of possible contamination, bias, outliers, or preparation issues.
Recommendations for corrective action and improved laboratory workflows.
Reports and Deliverables
QA/QC summary report with observations and conclusions.
Tables or charts showing control performance where applicable.
Data-quality comments for technical teams and project files.
Recommendations for future sampling and analytical programs.
