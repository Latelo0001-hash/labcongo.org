Activities Across the Mining Analysis Value Chain

LabCongo's activities cover the practical work needed to transform mining samples into reliable information: reception, preparation, analysis, validation, reporting, and client support.

Our daily work is organized around discipline, traceability, technical collaboration, and continuous improvement so that every analytical result can support better decisions.

Sample Preparation Activity

This activity covers the practical preparation steps that help samples move into analysis with better traceability and consistency.

Key details
  • Reception and organization of samples.
  • Preparation of representative laboratory portions.
  • Traceability checks before analytical work.
Laboratory Analysis Activity

This activity focuses on laboratory measurements and observations used to support mineral evaluation and technical reporting.

Key details
  • Analytical work adapted to the sample type.
  • Verification of results through laboratory controls.
  • Preparation of information for technical interpretation.
Training and Technical Exchange

This activity supports practical learning by connecting participants with laboratory methods, equipment use, and professional workflows.

Key details
  • Practical exposure to laboratory procedures.
  • Technical exchange with students, technicians, or partners.
  • Support for local capacity development.
Quality Control Review

This activity helps confirm that laboratory results are supported by clear records, quality checks, and traceable documentation.

Key details
  • Review of checks, records, and control information.
  • Verification of traceability before reporting.
  • Support for consistent and reliable data delivery.
Field and Client Support

This activity supports clients and partners as they define sample needs, choose analytical workflows, and interpret laboratory information.

Key details
  • Guidance before sample submission.
  • Support for analytical workflow selection.
  • Follow-up after results and reporting.
Environmental Monitoring Support

This activity supports mining-related environmental monitoring through disciplined laboratory handling and analytical support.

Key details
  • Support for environmental samples linked to mining activity.
  • Laboratory analysis for monitoring and follow-up needs.
  • Clear information for responsible decision-making.
