Partners Supporting Reliable Mining Analysis

LabCongo works with organizations that share the same commitment to reliable data, responsible mineral development, local capacity, and transparent laboratory practices.

Our partnerships are designed to strengthen the mining analysis ecosystem in the DRC through technical collaboration, practical training, quality control, equipment reliability, and project support.

Collaboration Framework

Every partnership should be practical, measurable, and connected to the quality of laboratory work or the development of local technical capacity.

Define the Need

We clarify the technical objective, partner expectations, available samples, reporting needs, and practical constraints.

Agree on Scope

We define responsibilities, timelines, quality expectations, communication points, and deliverables.

Execute the Work

The partnership moves through sample handling, laboratory activities, training sessions, or technical support work.

Review the Impact

We review results, lessons learned, follow-up needs, and opportunities for stronger collaboration.
